About Us

Who Are We? What Are Our Goals?

We are team number 7444 of the First Foundation's FRC. Our Ayyıldız Robotics Team was established in September 2018 with the partnership of the Electricity-Electronics, Machinery, and Motor Vehicles departments of our school, consisting of 10 students and 4 mentors. Our team, which quickly made a name for itself, has grown to 50 students as of 2024. Our team, established at Borusan Asım Kocabıyık Vocational Technical and Anatolian High School, continues its initiatives with the slogan "It won't end until you win." The championship we won in 2023 is one of our greatest motivations. Our main duty is to add experience and fun to our team day by day as we progress on the path of this championship.

What is Tulpar?

Tulpar holds an important place in Turkish mythology as a legendary creature. Depicted as a winged horse, Tulpar is a magnificent and powerful figure. However, Tulpar's wings are not visible to everyone; only heroes and noble-spirited individuals can see these wings. This special ability significantly enhances Tulpar's mystery and power. Tulpar symbolizes hope and courage, inspiring people in difficult times and encouraging them to overcome challenges. Tulpar's role in mythology goes beyond being just a physical entity. It is the most loyal companion of warriors and heroes, providing them with speed and endurance. In Turkish epics, heroes riding on Tulpar often achieve great victories and bring peace to their people. Thus, Tulpar is not only a mythological being but also a symbol embedded in a nation's collective memory. Tulpar's legend indicates the rich and deep roots of Turkish culture. This legendary horse not only physically carries people but also uplifts their spirits. Tulpar's wings are like a door always open to the sky, symbolizing hope, freedom, and grandeur. Moreover, as of 2024, our robot's name remains "Tulpar."
